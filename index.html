About me

I’m a junior in the Interior Design program at Meredith College, with minors in Marketing and Economics. My interest in interior design started with seeing how often spaces leave people out — and that’s what drives me to advocate for designs that work for everyone, at every ability.

At Meredith I’ve built a strong toolkit: Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, AutoCAD, SketchUp, hand drafting, and more. I use it to solve real design problems and keep growing so I can build a lasting career in interior design.
